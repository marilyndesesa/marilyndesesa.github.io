Hello, my name is Marilyn DeSesa. I am a senior software analyst at a fortune 500 company looking to break into the field of front end web development. I am constantly looking to expand my skill-set and practicing new ways to make sleek, responsive sites with a healthy balance between functionality and creative visual design.
